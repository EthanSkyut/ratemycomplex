Why we built this

Finding a place to live shouldn't feel like gambling. Between sketchy listings, unresponsive landlords, and deposits that mysteriously never come back, renters — especially students — get burned constantly, with no way to know what they're walking into beforehand. RateMyComplex started in one college town to fix that, and we're building it out for renters everywhere.
